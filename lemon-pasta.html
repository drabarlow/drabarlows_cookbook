Recipe 7 Lemon pasta

Another 15 min pasta meal, basically from store cupboard ingredients. Also works well as a light summer lunch

7.1 Ingredients

Serves a family, takes 15 mins

  • 500g pasta (dried)
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 50 ml olive oil
  • 3 large garlic cloves, crushed
  • 50g parmesan or Parmigiano Reggiano, grated (or as much as you want)
  • 60g rocket (optional)

To serve

  • More grated parmesan
  • Salt and black pepper

7.2 Method

  1. Boil pasta until al dente
  2. Meanwhile, gently heat the olive oil and add the garlic. You’re aiming to have the garlic just bubbling. Any more heat and it will burn and taste horrible
  3. When cooked, drain pasta collecting cooking water. Add lemon zest and juice, cheese, and the garlicky oil, stir
  4. Adjust sauce to desired consistency using pasta cooking water if required
  5. Add rocket to the hot pasta so it wilts a little. Serve!

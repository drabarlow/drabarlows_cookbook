Recipe 9 Charred Cherry Tomoatoes with Butter Beans

This is one of my favourite lunches. Eat while still warm or cold. The Butter bean puree is a revelation: super creamy and delicious with a range of things!

9.1 Ingredients

For the charred tomatoes (enough for 2 servings)

  • 325g cherry plum tomatoes
  • 50 ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (crushed)
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme (chopped)
  • Grated rind of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp dark brown sugar
  • 3/4 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • salt
  • black pepper

For butter beans

  • tin butter beans
  • olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt

To serve

  • Pitta

9.2 Method

First make charred tomatoes

  1. Put all charred tomato ingredients into a small oven-proof dish. Add salt and a good grind of pepper. Grill on max heat until the tomatoes start to blacken and char

Then the butter beans

  1. Drain the tin of bean reserving the liquid. Add beans, a dash of olive oil and salt to a food processor. Mix
  2. Slowly add the bean liquid and mix until a smooth puree is formed. Taste to check seasoning

Add beans to a dish and top with the tomatoes. Devour with hot pitta :)
